Deep In Motion : 7.3 Instructions for use and training materials

Created by PÃ¥l Haugen on 18.08.2025

As this is a prototype, formal Instructions for Use (IFU) and training materials have not yet been developed. However, the system already incorporates basic user guidance through the interface itself. A progress bar informs users about the current stage of processing (e.g., tracking, prediction, explainability), and explanatory text clarifies the meaning of results, such as the cutoff at 0.35 between high and low risk. In a future production system, these elements will be complemented with a structured IFU and training documentation, in line with MDR/IVDR requirements, ensuring that users are properly trained and equipped to operate the system safely.
