Deep In Motion : 12.4 Unit detection

Created by Felix Tempel on 09.07.2025

Values across sequences were normalized by infant-specific physical dimensions (trunk length) to bring all skeletal coordinates to a common scale.

This ensures comparability across body sizes and removes unit variability in keypoint distances.

Such normalization is critical in biomechanics and was implemented to ensure robustness to anatomical difference biases.
