Deep In Motion : 15.3 Choice of Epochs

Created by Felix Tempel, last updated on 17.06.2025
  • Training was conducted with a maximum of 100 epochs during Neural Architecture Search.
  • Early stopping was applied during Neural Architectural Search with a patience of 10 epochs, monitoring the validation AUC.
  • This mechanism ensured training was halted when performance no longer improved on the validation data.
  • The ten optimal architectures from Neural Architecture Search were subsequently trained for 200 epochs on each of the seven cross-validation folds, resulting in a total of 70 ensemble models.
