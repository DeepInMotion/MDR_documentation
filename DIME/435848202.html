Deep In Motion : 10.1: Datasets

Created by Daniel Groos, last updated on 19.08.2025

A total of 557 infants were included (418 for method development, 139 for external validation). Each infant contributed a video of spontaneous movements between 9–18 weeks corrected age.

Justification

  • Data collected from 13 hospitals in Belgium, India, Norway, and the US, ensuring demographic and clinical diversity.

  • Balanced allocation (75% development, 25% validation) aligns with best practice for machine-learning model evaluation.

  • 84 infants (15.1%) diagnosed with cerebral palsy provided an adequate event rate for statistical analysis.

  • External validation yielded robust results (Sensitivity 71.4%, Specificity 94.1%, Accuracy 90.6%).

  • Study conducted according to TRIPOD guidelines, ensuring methodological transparency.
