Deep In Motion : 2.1: Medical purpose (diagnosis, therapy, monitoring, predictions)

Created by Pål Haugen, last updated by Daniel Groos on 18.08.2025

Requirements

The manufacturer has determined for which medical purpose (diagnosis, therapy, monitoring, predictions) the medical product should support.

Definition

The product prototype is a software for early prediction of Cerebral Palsy (CP) in human infants. The infants are in the age of 9-18 weeks and at initial high-risk of developing CP according to specified medical criteria.
