Regulatory references

15.1

The manufacturer performs model training, tuning of hyperparameters and model selection exclusively with the training and validation data (e.g. using cross-validation).

The manufacturer should also show that the training actually improves the quality of the model.


15.2

The manufacturer tried different sets of hyperparameters and documented his final choice.23.



15.3

The manufacturer has documented the choice of epochs24.



15.4

The manufacturer has determined, documented and justified the quality dimensions on the basis of the intended use for which he wants to optimize the model.

This means that the choice of these quality parameters is specific to the intended use.


15.5

The manufacturer has - as far as reasonable - trained and compared several model types (including simpler and interpretable model).
