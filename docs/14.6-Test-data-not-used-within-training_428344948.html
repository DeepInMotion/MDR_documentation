Deep In Motion : 14.6 Test data not used within training

Created by Felix Tempel, last updated by Daniel Groos on 20.08.2025

Requirements

  • The manufacturer has documented how he ensures that test data are not used for model training and validation.   

Documentation

Dataset Partitioning

  • Development vs. Test: In (Groos et al.), a strict 75%/25% split at the infant level was enforced.

    • The 25% external validation set was held out and never exposed during model architecture search, hyperparameter tuning, or internal validation steps.

  • Internal Validation: Within the development set, only cross-validation folds were used for model selection and tuning. No test-set information was referenced during these steps.

  • Final Evaluation: After finalizing model(s) on development data, the ensemble was evaluated once on the held-out test set to obtain performance metrics.

Justification

  • To obtain unbiased estimates of generalization performance and comply with best practices in ML for regulated medical devices.

Regulatory References

  • ISO 13485: document procedures for design validation, ensuring unbiased evaluation; traceability of datasets to evaluation results.
