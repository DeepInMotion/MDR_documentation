Deep In Motion : 16.7 Ultimate choice of model

Created by Felix Tempel, last updated by Daniel Groos on 20.08.2025

Requirements

  •  The manufacturer has justified the ultimate choice of model on the basis of the quality criteria and the intended use, and has explained in particular when simpler and more interpretable models were not used.

Summary

Model selection rationale:

The ensemble model from Groos et al. was selected for the clinical study:

  • Already published 
  • Robust performance

The NAS model from Tempel et al. could be used for future improvements:

  • High sensitivity (76.2%), minimizing false negatives.

  • Low model complexity (0.6M parameters), enabling deployment in mobile and clinical settings.

  • Explainability potential (compact single model vs. large ensemble).
