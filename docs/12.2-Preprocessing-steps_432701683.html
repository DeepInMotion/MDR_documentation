Deep In Motion : 12.2 Preprocessing steps

Created by Felix Tempel, last updated by Daniel Groos on 20.08.2025

Requirements

  • This procedure describes the individual processing steps such as conversion, transformation, aggregation, normalization, format conversion, calculation of feature, conversion of numerical data into categories.

Summary

Individual steps in the procedure include:

  • Temporal resampling of skeletal time series (conversion).

  • Normalization using infant-specific trunk measurements.

  • Spatial rotation to align key body points (transformation).

  • Feature engineering: calculation of position, velocity, and relative distances between joints (e.g., bone lengths and angles).
