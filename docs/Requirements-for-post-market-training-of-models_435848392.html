Deep In Motion : Requirements for post-market training of models

Created by Espen Alexander Furst Ihlen, last updated by Lars Adde on 09.09.2025

Requirements

The manufacturer has specified whether the system should continue to learn after it has been placed on the market. If this is the case, the manufacturer has specified whether this continuous training will be global/centralized or decentralized (e.g., per product or per hospital) and online or offline.

Descriptions

Dependent of specification of intended user, user context, core tasks (i.e. detail description of section 3). Should comply with standards (see e.g. IEC 62304: Medical Device Software LifeCycle Processes | by Dr Stephen Odaibo | The Blog of RETINA-AI Health, Inc. | Medium)

The system will be set up with agreements between manufacturer and hospital to have access to 2 year follow-up cerebral palsy status collected from medical records for post-market verification and retraining of models. The system will retrain models after it has been placed on the market and the training will be decentralized at the local hospital offline.
