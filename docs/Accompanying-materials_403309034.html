2017/745/EU, Annex I, chap. III, 23.1, 23.4., 21 CFR part 801, 21 CFR part 814.20, XAVIER: "Perspectives and Good Practices for AI and Continuously Learning Systems in Healthcare", ISO 24028, XAVIER University "Building Explainability and Trust for AI in Healthcare", ISO 24028 e.g. 10.11.3
