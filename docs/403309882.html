The manufacturer has described the core tasks that the medical device is to support.


Core tasks dependent on definitions of intended user and user context to be described in detail 

Description

The core task of the medical device is to support desicion making about risk for cerebral palsy in the described intended use environment (Table 3.2) and intended use (Table 3.1). The medical device will be one out of several assessment tools bringing desicion support to the team in the intended use environment and the final intended user, the medical doctor. The medical doctor will use the medical device output results ""at high risk for CP/at low risk for CP" as one result among many others deciding an possible overall clinical "at high risk for CP/at low risk for CP" desicion. The core task of the medical device is to contribute with one CP risk classification into the overall desicion finally made by the medical doctor as the intended user.
