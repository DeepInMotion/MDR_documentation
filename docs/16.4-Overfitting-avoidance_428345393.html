Deep In Motion : 16.4 Overfitting avoidance

Created by Felix Tempel on 17.06.2025

Overfitting was addressed through multiple mechanisms:

Early stopping criteria:

  • Training was halted at epoch 6 if the AUC was below 0.5 (Tempel et al.).

  • Final model training ran for 300 epochs with learning rate halving at epochs 200 and 250 to help convergence.

Cross-validation:

  • 7-fold stratified CV was used to verify generalization across training and validation subsets.

Augmentation:

  • Input skeletons were augmented via rotation, scaling, and translation during training to increase data variability and model robustness.

Evidence:

  • Test set performance (sensitivity 76.2%, specificity 93.2%) was close to CV results, suggesting no overfitting to training data occurred.
