Deep In Motion : 3.1: Characteristics of intended users

Created by Espen Alexander Furst Ihlen, last updated by Lars Adde on 15.09.2025

Requirements

The manufacturer has characterized the intended users, e.g. using demographic features (age, gender), regarding the training, experience in medical domains, regarding technical knowledge, physical and mental limitations, linguistic skills and cultural background.

Description

The intended user of the medical device output results is a medical doctor whithin the medical domain assessing infants below 6 month post term age for their development. There is no specific need for perticular expereince in the medical domain, technical knowledge or additional training (reading the instructions for use is considered sufficient). The software will support the intended user in the decision of an interim diagnosis of cerebral palsy before 5 months post-term age. Intended user of the software calculating the output result is healthcare personnel like a physiotherapist and/or nurse within the medical domain assessing infants below 6 months post term age for their development. There is no specific need for perticular expereince in the medical domain, technical knowledge or additional training beyond reading the instructions for use that is considered sufficient to use the software.
