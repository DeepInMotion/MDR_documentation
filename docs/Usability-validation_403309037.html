As part of the usability validation, the manufacturer assesses whether users understand the instructions for use.

This includes other accompanying materials.

As part of the usability validation, the manufacturer evaluates whether the users blindly trust the product or verify the results.

20.3

As part of the usability validation, the manufacturer evaluates whether users correctly recognize and understand the results.

This also includes the display of errors and the explanations ("explainability"). Manufacturers should include all safety-related use scenarios in the validation and all risk-minimizing measures on the interface.

IEC 62366-1, 5.7-5.9
