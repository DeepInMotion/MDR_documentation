D

Justification

  • Representativeness: ensures that the model sees diverse data during training and that external validation is meaningful across all strata.

  • Mitigating site bias: controls for site-specific characteristics (video quality, population differences, etc.) so that performance metrics generalize.

  • Outcome balance: preserves CP vs. non-CP ratio and subtype proportions, critical when prevalence is relatively low (15%).
