Deep In Motion : Requirements for the input data

Created by Espen Alexander Furst Ihlen, last updated by Lars Adde on 09.09.2025

Requirements

The manufacturer has specified the requirements for the input data.

Description

Dependent of specification of intended user, user context, core tasks (i.e. detail description of section 3). Should comply with standards (see e.g. IEC 62304: Medical Device Software LifeCycle Processes | by Dr Stephen Odaibo | The Blog of RETINA-AI Health, Inc. | Medium)

 

The software takes as input videos of spontaneous movements at 10 to 18 weeks post term age in infants that the medical doctor considers having an increased risk for cerebral palsy. The videos are recorded by medical personnel or by other persons (e.g. parents) under guidance by medical personnel in the outpatient clinic or at home. The videos must comply with the following standards: 1-5 minutes length, min 30 frames per second, min 480 pixels in hight and /or width, no zooming, no other objects moving in video frame, infant wearing a nappy and/or a body, normal room light, infant being awake and comfortable, infant not using pacifier, infant in supine position and whole body visible in video frame.
